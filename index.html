About Us

Arena Animation GTB Nagar is a creative institute focused on animation, VFX, graphic design and web development training. It has been providing animation and multimedia education for around 30 years. Its branches are located in major cities like Delhi, Kolkata, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Gurgaon, Jaipur and many more. The institue operates through 200+ centers in India and has an international presence in over 20 countries. We help students learn practical skills through real projects, creative activities and industry-based training programs.

Our experienced mentors guide students to build strong portfolios and prepare for creative careers in media and design industries.

Arena Animation GTB Nagar also provides placement assistance and career guidance to help students explore opportunities in animation, VFX, graphic design and digital media industries. Students get a friendly learning environment with modern tools, creative workshops and hands-on experience.
